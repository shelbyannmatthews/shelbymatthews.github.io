Moose Carriage

Illustrator & After Effects Animation

"Moose Carriage" is an animation inspired by my love for moose and the pop surrealist artistry of Mark Ryden. Ryden's works incorporate children's colors and aesthetics,
which are well reflected in this piece. Every component of this piece was illustrated in Adobe Illustrator and animated with Adobe After Effects to bring the visuals to life.
