Travelling Through Bear Creek

Illustrator & After Effects Animation

After successfully animating "Moose Carriage", a better developed storyline was in order. "Bear Creek" tells the story of Mason Moose visiting
Bear Creek Rd, about to park his carriage. However, he hates rain and a storm is settling in. Mason decides to leave his carriage and travel in all new directions,
avoiding the rain. He eventually makes it back to his carriage and accidentally crashes into it.

Nearly every animated scene in "Bear Creek" is illustrated in Adobe Illustrator. The scenes with 4k video were taken from a personal drone.
This movie is inspired by Mark Ryden's children's aesthetics and Wes Anderson's cinematic talents. Inspiration from their works shows
in the limited color palettes, camera work, and perspectives.
