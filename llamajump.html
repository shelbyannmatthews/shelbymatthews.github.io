Llama Jump

Game Design, Unity & C#

Llama Jump is a platform game, requiring the player (farmer) to navigate its world to retrieve a runaway llama. The player must use WASD or
the arrow keys to move and use the space bar to jump. My responsibilities as the artist in this project are to create the sprites
for the farmer, animals, and illustrations of the platforms and barn.
