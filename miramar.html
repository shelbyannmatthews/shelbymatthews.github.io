USMC Stamps

Photography, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

These USMC stamps were designed using original photos from the Marine Corps flight demonstration at the San Diego Miramar Airshow.
Each photo of the aircraft was masked in Photoshop and edited with texture and color to obtain the appearance of a stamp. The stamp shape
was created in Adobe Illustrator and then imported into Photoshop, where the aircraft images were integrated. All fonts, colors,
and logos align with the official Marine Corps brand guidelines.
