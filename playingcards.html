Mason Moose's Playing Cards

Illustrator & Dimension

Mason Moose's Playing Cards is a conceptual project I created to get children interested in playing physical games again. So many
children are too involved with video games, so I wanted to bring back something more hands-on. The game set includes a standard deck, Go Fish,
and a memory game to help young children connect with others and and have fun with animals.

All of these illustrations were completed on Adobe Illustrator and imported to Adobe Dimension to create a realistic mockup of the product.
